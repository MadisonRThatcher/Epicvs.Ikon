Question 1¶

Does it snow more in the US or Canada?¶

Summary: Determine which country expirences more snow in their ski resorts.
Hypothesis: There is more snow in Canada than in the US.

In [17]:
import StatTests
cots=StatTests.MeansTest(Df_ski,'Country','Total_Snowfall')
cots

The groups are Canada and US

This is a test of equal variances with Ho: The variances are equal
The t test statistic is 0.506 and the p-value is 0.4769
Conclusion: Fail to Reject Ho: We can't reject that the variances are the same

This is a Two-Sample t test (assuming equal variances) of equal means with Ho: The group means are equal
The t test statistic is -1.266 and the p-value is 0.2096
Conclusion: Fail to Reject Ho: We can't reject that the means are the same

Conclusion:¶

The p-value is equal to 0.2096 which is larger than 0.05. Therefore we must fail to reject the null hypothesis.
The snowfall amount in Canada is not more than the snowfall amount in the US.
